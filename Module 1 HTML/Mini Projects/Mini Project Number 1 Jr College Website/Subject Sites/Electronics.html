Course Description

This course presents an overview of the fundamentals of electric/electronic circuit analysis, starting with an overview of electrical theory and moving to simple circuit components like power supplies, resistors, capacitors, and inductors..

Course Outcome

On successful completion of this program, students would be able to:

  • To give knowledge of some basic electronic components and circuits.
  • To introduce basics of diode and transistor circuits
  • To expose the students to working of some power electronic dev ices, transducers and application of transducers.
  • To introduce basic aspect of electronic communication systems.
  • The associated Laboratory Practical course is designed to understand working of various Electronic circuits. The students will u understand how to u se the basic test and measuring instruments to test the circuits.
