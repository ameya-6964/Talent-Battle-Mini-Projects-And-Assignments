Course Description

Get an insight into the world of computers, their applications and the latest development within the industry through professional engagements and hands-on sessions that sync with the ever-changing business environment. Explore the various conceptual underpinnings of Computer Science such as fundamental algorithms, data structures, programming languages, automation theory, computer organization, database, operating systems, and software engineering techniques.

Course Outcome

On successful completion of this program, students would be able to:

  • Develop ability to analyze a problem, identify and define the computing requirements, which may be appropriate to its solution.
  • To prepare students to undertake careers involving problem solving using computer science and technologies.
  • Develop ability to pursue advanced studies and research in computer science. To produce entrepreneurs who can innovate and develop software product.
