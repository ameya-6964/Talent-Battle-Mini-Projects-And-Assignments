College Description

The college was started in 2001 in the premises of the New English School, Kalwa. Now, the same is situated in Airoli from 2003 with a full-fledged independent building and Jr. College started in the year 2017. College is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and the Govt. of Maharashtra has given due permission to run it on permanently Unaided Basis. All infrastructural facilities have been provided in the college building and due care has been taken to appoint highly qualified teaching staff. The college carefully follows all the rules and regulations of the Universityof Mumbai and Govt. of Maharashtra. The college has various faculties from Jr. College to regular graduation & Post Graduation courses in the field of Science & Commerce. Specialised courses are also being conducted in the field of Information Technology, Accounts & Finance , Banking & Insurance, Management Studies and Mass Media. College is accredited with 'A' Grade by NAAC second cycle with 3.33 Score

Vision

To make the institution a centre of academic excellence. To ensure that quality education is accessible to all. To tune-in the minds of youth for an ever-changing world.

Mission

To empower students with knowledge and skill. To develop competence for gainful employment. To inculcate discipline, commitment and sensitivity.
